Frequently Asked Questions

What can Wright do?

Wright is an AI assistant for macOS. It provides three basic functions you can trigger from any app via keyboard shortcuts or PopClip:

  • Rewrite ‚Äî Polish selected text for grammar, tone, and clarity while keeping your original meaning.
  • Translate ‚Äî Translate selected text between languages instantly.
  • Chat ‚Äî Ask questions and have a conversation with AI, with the option to transition from a quick Quick View to a full Chat interface.

Which AI models are supported?

Wright is powered by leading AI models:

  • OpenAI ChatGPT
  • Google Gemini
  • Grok (xAI)
  • Groq

What are Custom AI Roles?

Custom Roles let you create specialized AI personas with their own system prompts. For example, you can set up a "Code Reviewer" role, a "Copywriter" role, or a "Translator" role ‚Äî each behaves differently based on the instructions you define. Switch between roles instantly from the Quick View or Chat.

How does PopClip integration work?

If you have PopClip installed, Wright adds actions to PopClip's popup menu. Select text in any app, and PopClip will show options to rewrite, translate, or chat ‚Äî no keyboard shortcut needed. You can download the PopClip extension from Wright's Settings.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Wright does not collect or store your personal data. Your text is processed by the AI and the result is returned directly ‚Äî Wright does not log or retain your content. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What are the system requirements?

Wright requires macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later. It needs Accessibility permissions to read and replace selected text system-wide. The app is lightweight and runs as a menu bar app.

How do keyboard shortcuts work?

Wright uses global keyboard shortcuts that work in any app. The default shortcuts can be customized in Settings ‚Üí Shortcuts. Select text anywhere, press the shortcut, and the result appears instantly ‚Äî you can copy, insert, or expand into a Chat session.
