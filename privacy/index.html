Privacy Policy for Wright

Last Updated: February 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

Wright is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe in transparency and minimize data collection to the absolute essentials.

Key Privacy Points:
  • We do not access or store your conversation content
  • We only collect your email for account management
  • We use minimum connections for service delivery

Data Collection & Architecture

Wright utilizes a secure backend API service to process your requests. We do not store your conversation history or content.

Service Architecture

All interactions are processed through our secure API. Your content is passed through solely for generating responses and is never saved.

  • We collect only your account email
  • Minimum data used for troubleshooting
  • No retention of conversation data
  • Secure implementation

Third-Party Services

When you use Wright to interact with LLM services (such as OpenAI, Groq, etc.), your interactions are governed by the privacy policies of those services. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of the LLM services you choose to use:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Groq Privacy Policy
  • xAI Privacy Policy

Data Security

Your account information is stored securely. We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
