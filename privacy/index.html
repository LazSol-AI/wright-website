Privacy Policy for Wright

Last Updated: March 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

Wright is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe in transparency and are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information.

Key Privacy Points:
  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not track your usage
  • We do not store any of your data

Data Collection and Storage

Wright does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about our users. The app operates entirely on your local device.

API Keys

The only data you need to provide is your API key for the Language Learning Model (LLM) services you wish to use. These API keys are:

  • Stored securely in your macOS Keychain
  • Never transmitted to our servers
  • Only used to communicate directly with your chosen LLM services
  • Can be removed at any time through the app or your Keychain Access

Third-Party Services

When you use Wright to interact with LLM services (such as OpenAI, Groq, etc.), your interactions are governed by the privacy policies of those services. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of the LLM services you choose to use:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Groq Privacy Policy
  • DeepSeek Privacy Policy

Data Security

Your API keys are stored securely in the macOS Keychain, which provides encrypted storage for sensitive data. This is the same system that macOS uses to store passwords and other secure information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
